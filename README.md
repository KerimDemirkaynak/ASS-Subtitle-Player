# ASS Subtitle Player

## Summary
Play local .ass and .srt subtitles on any web video. Optimized for HiAnime with a sleek, modern UI and aspect ratio support.

---

## Description

Enhance your streaming experience with **ASS Subtitle Player**! This lightweight and powerful extension allows you to overlay your own local subtitle files onto any web-based video player.

Whether you are watching anime on **HiAnime** or enjoying content on other streaming platforms, this extension ensures you can use your preferred subtitles with professional-grade rendering.

### ✨ Key Features:
* **Format Support:** Full compatibility with **.ass**, **.ssa**, and **.srt** files.
* **HiAnime Optimized:** Specially designed to work within iframes, ensuring the subtitle picker triggers correctly even on complex video hosting sites.
* **Modern Popup UI:** A clean, distraction-free interface. No more messy buttons cluttering the video page—everything is managed from the extension panel.
* **Aspect Ratio Toggle:** Easily switch between **16:9** and **4:3** modes to prevent subtitles from overlapping with black bars.
* **Clear Rendering:** High-quality subtitle rendering with outlines and shadows for maximum readability.
* **Mobile Friendly:** Fully compatible with **Firefox for Android**.
* **Privacy First:** No data collection. Your subtitle files are processed entirely locally in your browser.

### 🚀 How to Use:
1. Navigate to any website with a video player (e.g., HiAnime).
2. Click the **ASS Subtitle Player** icon in your browser toolbar.
3. Click the **"📁 Select Subtitle"** button.
4. Choose your subtitle file from your device.
5. Sit back and enjoy!

### 💡 Why ASS Subtitle Player?
Many extensions struggle with modern video sites that use nested iframes. **ASS Subtitle Player** uses a smart broadcasting system to detect the video player wherever it is hidden on the page, providing a seamless "plug-and-play" experience.

---

**Permissions Note:**
* `activeTab` & `scripting`: Required to render subtitles over the video.
* `storage`: Used to remember your "Enable" and "Aspect Ratio" preferences.
